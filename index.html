Schweizer Bahnhofsuhr
The Swiss Federal Railways Clock

This widget reproduces the mechanical subtleties of the Swiss Federal Railways clock, the "Schweizer Bahnhofsuhr", by modeling each hand as a damped torsional harmonic oscillator.

The clock was designed by Swiss engineer and designer Hans Hilfiker in 1944, while working for the Swiss Federal Railways.
